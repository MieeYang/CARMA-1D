This routine drives the vertical transport calculations.

NOTE: Since this is only for sedimentation and brownian diffusion of a column within a parent model, the advection of air density, gases and potential temperature have been removed. Also, the divergence corrections (divcor) for 1D transport are not applied, since these columns exist within a parent model that is responsible for the advection.
